# Summary of Findings

- The dataset contains 23,335 NBA games from 2004–2020.
- Home teams win more than 50% of games, but home-court advantage has declined since 2014.
- Three-point shooting percentages have increased steadily over time.
- From 2018 onward, 25 team-seasons shot ≥ 37% from three.
- Only 2 high-accuracy teams still had losing records.
- 7 low-accuracy teams (≤ 34%) had losing records.
- Higher 3-point accuracy strongly correlates with winning, but defense, paint scoring, ball movement, and free throws also matter.
